Bunny Breakfast

Come to the Bunny Breakfast on Saturday March 28th (8am-11am) at the Ozaukee Fairgrounds Pavilion (click to see map). The event will include:

  • An opportunity for you to take photos with the Bunny
  • Spring crafts & activities
  • Live bunnies and chicks
  • Pancakes, ham, sausages, fruit, applesauce, ice cream, & muffins
  • And so much more!

The cost is only $7.50 for adults, $5.50 for 3 to 12 year olds (2 and under free)! Purchase tickets online or at Port Washington State Bank locations before March 23rd.

All tickets for the 2015 Bunny Breakfast have been sold.
Watch for the event again in 2016!
